about us

why choose us?

Ataffysa's bakery always makes improvements by building new factories and using more modern machinery and equipment facilities to produce quality, safe, and halal products. It has also obtained a Halal Certificate from MUI, and a distribution permit from BPOM RI. With the recognition of the certification body, Ataffysa's bakery will be more optimistic in facing the future.

Established on May 1, 2005, Ataffysa’s bakery started small-scale bakery and cake production. To meet the increasing demand for bakery & cake markets, Ataffysa’s bakery made improvements by building a new factory and using more modern machinery and equipment facilities to produce quality, safe, and halal products. In addition, improving the quality of human resources is also carried out to keep up with the development of the company and answer customer needs.

vision

We are the partner of choice and the leading quality company in the bakery and confectionery business. Together with our customers, we explore the world of baking and confectionery, constantly seeking new opportunities and ingredients to improve products and processes.
